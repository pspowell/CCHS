## What We Aim to Achieve

This coalition is dedicated to four clear objectives:

### **1. Transparency**
Ensuring members have access to board minutes, rosters, financial reports, and other documents guaranteed by law and bylaws.

### **2. Accountability**
Ensuring board actions follow Ohio law, nonprofit best practices, and the Society's own bylaws.

### **3. Member Voice**
Restoring the active role of members in decisions, elections, and the direction of the organization.

### **4. Organizational Renewal**
